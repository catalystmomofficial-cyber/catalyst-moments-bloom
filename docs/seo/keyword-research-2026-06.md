# Catalyst Mom — Keyword Research & Topic Clusters (June 2026)

Method: SERP-based clustering per the `seo-cluster` skill — keywords grouped by
who actually ranks in the live top 10, intent-classified, and mapped to a
hub-and-spoke architecture around existing site assets (birth ball hub, Core
Restore Foundations program, meal plans, workout plans).

Competitiveness is judged from SERP composition (who ranks today), not paid
volume data. Volume/difficulty numbers require a DataForSEO or Ahrefs key.

---

## Cluster 1 — Birth Ball (existing hub: strongest asset, expand it)

The SERP is winnable: ranked by mom blogs and brand sites (BabyGo, Trideer,
thebirthball.com, MamasteFit, Nourish Move Love), not just medical institutions.
The site already has 10+ birth ball pages — this is the cluster to press.

| Keyword | Intent | SERP difficulty | Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| birth ball exercises pregnancy | Informational | Medium | Strengthen existing BirthBallExercise page (NHS + Tommy's rank, but so do blogs) |
| birth ball to induce labor | Informational/high traffic | Low-Medium | **Gap** — dedicated page; current EarlyLabor page doesn't target "induce" phrasing |
| what size birthing ball for my height | Commercial | Low | Make BirthBallBuyingGuide answer the height table directly (48-55cm ≤5'3", 58-65cm 5'4"-5'9", 68-75cm ≥5'10") |
| birth ball positions for labor | Informational | Low-Medium | Spoke post; small blogs rank |
| birthing ball third trimester / 32 weeks | Informational | Low | Maps to existing BirthBallTrimester page — add week-specific sections |
| peanut ball vs birth ball | Commercial/comparison | Low | **Gap** — comparison post, only niche tool sites rank |
| is bouncing on a birth ball safe during pregnancy | Informational (PAA) | Low | FAQ page addition |

## Recommended priority order

1. **Birth ball gap-fill** (induce-labor page, peanut ball comparison, size-guide
   optimization) — existing topical authority, lowest difficulty, fastest wins.
2. **Diastasis recti pillar + spokes** — direct revenue path to Core Restore
   Foundations; one blog (NML) proves the SERP is takeable.
3. **Breastfeeding meal plan pillar** — feeds the MealPlan product.
4. **Postpartum workout plan** — plan-format content around the medical SERPs.
5. **Self-care listicles** — cheap top-of-funnel volume, internal-link them into
   clusters 2-4.

## Internal linking matrix (hub-and-spoke)

- Birth ball pages → BirthBallBuyingGuide (commercial) and BirthBallFAQ.
- All diastasis/core spokes → diastasis pillar → Core Restore Foundations program page.
- Nutrition spokes → breastfeeding meal plan pillar → MealPlan tool.
- Self-care posts → cross-link into postpartum workout + nutrition pillars.
- Every postpartum post links the pelvic-floor spoke (shared subtopic).
